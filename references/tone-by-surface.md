## Notes on Jargon
- **Buzzwords**: Never. They're off-putting and come across as know-it-all.
- **Technical terms**: Context-dependent. One person's jargon is another's professional vocabulary. Know your audience and meet them there.
- **General rule**: If a term is precise and your audience knows it, use it. If it's vague or imprecise, cut it.
